Brownies

Description: The most incredible brownies you can make for the best bang for your buck

Ingredients needed:

  • 2 eggs + water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetend cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls
  2. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
  3. Pour the batter into an 8x8 inch baking pan liined with parchment paper
  4. Finally, bake!
